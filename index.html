About

Welcome to Freshbox Farm

The average kilogram of mealie meal or vegetable or meat that reaches your table can’t be grown without chemical fertilizers and herbicides or antibiotics for meat. Each year farmers are forced to increase these chemical inputs to maintain the yield, yet selling prices remain hovering around the same ranges. The trace elements that we ingest from the meals can cause untold damage to human health over time. The increasing levels of fertilizers in the soil and water bodies is also a worry. These are not bad in of themselves, the excessive use is our biggest problem. We at Fresh Box Direct intend to deliver food to you that is less dependent on synthetic inputs promoting good health and minimal adverse impact on the environment.
