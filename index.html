About

Welcome to Freshbox Farm

The average kilogram of mealie meal or vegetable or meat that reaches your table can’t be grown without chemical fertilisers and herbicides or antibiotics for meat. Each year farmers are forced to increase these chemical inputs to maintain the yield, yet selling prices remain hovering around the same ranges. The trace elements that we ingest from the meals can cause untold damage to human health over time. The increasing levels of fertilisers in the soil and water bodies is also a worry. These are not bad in and of themselves, the excessive use is our biggest problem. At Fresh Box Direct we deliver produce that is dependent on organic inputs. Your good health and a thriving environment is our priority. Our method is simple and proven. We keep animals that produce good meat for you and natural fertiliser for the food you eat. By growing it all ourselves and selling it you we can guarantee good food that is safe to consume. We partner with like-minded farmers to meet a variety your needs.
